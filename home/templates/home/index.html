Throughout the year new limited editions for the online shop go live. To give creativity no limitations each collection varies in shape, style, products and colors. Course Overview

Since 2018 Lotta has been passing on her knowledge about the making of pots. Next to her workshops, where group classes of up to five as well as one on one sessions in hand building and on the potters wheel take place. The courses focuses on the people and can therefore be freely designed to the wishes of the customers after discussion with Lotta.
